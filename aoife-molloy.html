Aoife Molloy

(EE-fuh MOL-oy)

Book(s):

Aoife is first introduced in Binding 13 and Keeping 13 as Joey Lynch's girlfriend and a minor character. Her full story is told in Saving 6 and Redeeming 6, and she later appears briefly in Taming 7 with Joey.

Relationships:

  • Son: AJ Lynch
  • Friends: Casey Lordan, Katie Wilmot, Shannon Lynch
  • Boyfriend: Joey Lynch

Background:

Aoife and Joey both attended Ballylaggin Community School (BCS) before transferring to Tommen College. Aoife met Joey on the first day of school at BCS and supported him through his addictions and abuse, becoming his best friend before they began dating.
