Aoife Molloy

(EE-fuh MOL-oy)

Book(s):

Aoife is first introduced in Binding 13 and Keeping 13 as Joey Lynch's girlfriend and a minor character. Her full story is told in Saving 6 and Redeeming 6, and she later appears briefly in Taming 7 with Joey.

Relationships:

  • Son: AJ Lynch
  • Friends: Casey Lordan, Katie Wilmot, Shannon Lynch
  • Boyfriend: Joey Lynch

Background:

Aoife and Joey both attended Ballylaggin Community School (BCS) before transferring to Tommen College. Aoife met Joey on the first day of school at BCS and supported him through his addictions and abuse, becoming his best friend before they began dating.

Notable Quotes from the Book(s):

  • "When I was with her, I felt like I was drowning and breathing all at once."
  • "'Don’t hate me, Molloy,' Joey mumbled, falling into the passenger seat the moment I let him go to open the car door. 'You’re all I have to wake up for in the morning.'"
  • "Don’t worry, Peter Pan. I’ll be your Wendy."
  • "All I could see was her. The girl from the wall."
  • "I love you more than I have ever loved another person in my life, and that’s not an exaggeration. That’s the god-honest truth."
