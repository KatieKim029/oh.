Evangeline Fox

(eh-VAN-juh-LEEN f-ock-s)

Book(s):

Once Upon a Broken Heart, The Ballad of Never After, and A Curse for True Love by Stephanie Garber all follow Evangeline. She is also mentioned by Donatella and Scarlett Dragna in Finale by Stephanie Garber of the Caraval series. She will also be a prominent character in the Mirror of Infinite Endings (cover to be revealed), a novella that will come out this fall!

Relationships:

  • Friend: Ariel (LaLa) Lagrimas, Donatella Dragna, Scarlett Dragna
  • Stepsister (relationship of dramatic irony unfortunately): Marisol Tourmaline
  • Boyfriend: Jacks of the Hollow

Background:

Evangeline, a cheerful and optimistic pink-haired girl, strikes a deal with the Prince of Hearts, Jacks, in order to prevent her step-sister from marrying the "love of her life," Luc. Since her parents died, her step-mother forced her to give up her work at her father's curiosity shop. She lives in the same universe as the Caraval series, eventually falling in love with Jacks (I'm pretty sure that's not a spoiler because if you search up Once Upon a Broken Heart, there's like a 95% chance you'll see something about Jacks.)

Notable Quotes from the Book(s):

  • "I believe there are far more possibilities than happily ever after or tragedy. Every story has the potential for infinite endings."
  • "I don't know if I can fix your broken heart, but you can take mine because it's already yours."
  • "And he loved her. He loved her. He loved her. He loved her. He loved her so much he’d rewritten history."
  • "Once upon a time, a girl who believed in fairytales stole the heart of a prince who had sworn to never love."
  • "Jacks had so badly wanted to tell her that he couldn't even remember what Donatella looked like, that Evangeline's face was the only one he saw whenever he closed his eyes, that he would go with her anywhere...if he could."
